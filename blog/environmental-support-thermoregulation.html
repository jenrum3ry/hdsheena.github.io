Misconception: Sweaters/booties = pampered dogs.
Reality: Thermoregulation = medical necessity for geriatric patients.

Aging causes sarcopenia (muscle wasting). Muscle generates heat + insulation. Lost muscle = lost warmth. Compromised respiratory systems + obesity = heat intolerance.

Shivering = burning irreplaceable calories. Excessive panting = fragile heart stress. Comfort = metabolic energy management.

Footwear: Traction & Protection

For DM or generalized weakness: traction (prevent splay) + abrasion resistance (prevent bleeding from dragging).

The Gear: Neopaws

No cheap balloons/thin socks. Need orthopedic-grade.

  • Neopaws Summer (Mesh) - Breathable, heavy-duty rubber sole, protects hot pavement, grip. Essential for dragging/knuckling on concrete (prevents nail grinding to quick)
  • Neopaws Winter (Neoprene) - Insulation, waterproof. Prevents snow ice balls between toes. Required below freezing with reduced circulation

Heat Management (Winter & Night)

Arthritic joints stiffen in cold. Cold dog = painful dog.

Heated Bed

  • Electric heated pet mat (chew-resistant cords)
  • Why: Sedentary seniors don't generate heat through movement. Passive heat prevents overnight stiffness
  • Protocol: On top of orthopedic bed. Ensure "cool zone" available

Wearables

  • Indoors: Fleece PJs/onesies - 24/7 insulation for sarcopenic dogs. Keeps hips/spine warm, synovial fluid less viscous
  • Outdoors: Insulated parkas (e.g., Hurtta) - Rule: You need coat = they need coat

Cooling Management (Summer)

Heatstroke kills faster than cold. Declining laryngeal function = less effective panting.

Cooling Vest

  • Mechanism: Evaporative cooling. Soak vest; evaporation pulls heat from core
  • Limit: Only works low-moderate humidity. High humidity = wet blanket
  • Protocol: Keep wet. Re-wet every 30min. Dry = insulator = cooks dog

Critical: Dry Vest = Heat Trap

Dry cooling vest traps heat. Cannot commit to 30min re-wetting? Remove it. Dry vest in heat = hyperthermia.

Cooling Mat (Gel)

  • Pressure-activated gel = instant relief (~20min)
  • Placement: Car + living room

Environmental Audit

Assess house as hazard zone:

  1. Floor slippery? → Boots/runners
  2. Draft cold? → Heated bed/PJs
  3. Pavement hot? → Summer boots

Not dressing up = managing environment their body cannot.
